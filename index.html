VibeLoop · Early access

Browser macros for teams that understand your data.

VibeLoop turns repetitive browser workflows into data-aware macros your team can actually rely on — for things like building lead lists from directories, pulling KPIs from dashboards, and checking portals on a schedule.

Where VibeLoop helps first
  • Turn Clutch/G2-style directories into ready-to-email Google Sheets without endless copy-paste.
  • Pull key numbers from logged-in dashboards (Stripe, CRMs, custom tools) into a weekly report.
  • Check internal portals or queues and flag what’s stuck or at-risk for your team.
